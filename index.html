SPACE GAME.

In a simple space shooter game, the player controls a spaceship that moves horizontally along the bottom of the screen. Movement is done using the directional keys on the keyboard, typically the left and right arrows or the A and D keys. The spaceship can fire projectiles upward by pressing the spacebar. The objective of the game is to destroy enemy aliens that appear at the top of the screen and slowly descend toward the spaceship. Each time a projectile hits an alien, the alien is destroyed and the player earns points. If an alien hits the spaceship or reaches the bottom of the screen, the player loses a life. The game may include progressively harder levels or feature endless point accumulation. Gameplay relies on quick reflexes and precise shooting.
